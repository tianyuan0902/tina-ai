# Clarity Creator

## Archetype
The operator who turns ambiguity, noise, and founder fragments into clearer direction.

## Best Fit
Early teams where the pain is known but the role, process, or product lane is still unresolved.

## Risk Pattern
May be undervalued if the founder thinks they only need execution capacity. This person creates leverage by shaping the problem before executing against it.

## Signals
- Simplifies complexity without flattening nuance.
- Notices what is unknown and keeps momentum anyway.
- Converts vague founder intent into sharper tradeoffs.
- Helps the team make decisions faster.

## Tina Judgment
This is often the profile founders need when they say "we just need help," but cannot yet define the exact role.
