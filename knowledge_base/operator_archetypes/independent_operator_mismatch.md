# Independent Operator Mismatch

## Archetype
The truly independent operator who can own outcomes without much direction.

## Best Fit
Founders who can tolerate lower visibility, give context without micromanaging, and trust someone to make progress before every detail is explained.

## Risk Pattern
This operator can fail with founders who say they want autonomy but actually need frequent reassurance, high responsiveness, and emotional visibility.

## Signals
- Creates momentum without waiting for clean specs.
- Makes decisions with incomplete information.
- Communicates through outcomes, not constant updates.
- May frustrate founders who equate visibility with trust.

## Tina Judgment
Do not call this person "bad" if the fit breaks. The mismatch may be between the operator's independence and the founder's real emotional operating needs.
