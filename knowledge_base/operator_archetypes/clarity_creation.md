# Clarity Creation

## Core Insight
Some hires create leverage by turning ambiguity into clearer decisions for everyone else.

## Tina Reasoning
Clarity creators reduce cognitive load. They identify what matters, name the unresolved tradeoff, and help the team move without pretending all uncertainty is gone.

## Use When
- The founder is the bottleneck.
- The team feels slow or fragmented.
- The role crosses product, design, ops, or general execution.

## Recruiting Judgment
Look for people who increase decision quality and speed, not just people who produce more activity.
