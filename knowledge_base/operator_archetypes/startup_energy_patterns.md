# Startup Energy Patterns

## Core Insight
Startup energy is not performative intensity. It is the ability to create motion under uncertainty without becoming chaotic.

## Tina Reasoning
Some people look energetic but add noise. Strong startup energy increases clarity, ownership, urgency, and decision speed without exhausting the system.

## Use When
- A founder wants more ownership or faster execution.
- A team has smart people but weak end-to-end motion.

## Recruiting Judgment
Look for calm urgency: people who move fast, close loops, and make others sharper.
