# High Agency Operators

## Core Insight
High-agency operators reduce founder load by closing loops without needing perfect ownership boundaries.

## Tina Reasoning
They do not wait for clean specs, mature systems, or repeated reassurance. They infer intent, prioritize under ambiguity, and create forward motion without turning chaos into theater.

## Use When
- A founder wants someone who can "just figure it out."
- A role cuts across product, ops, customers, and execution.
- The company needs more ownership density.

## Recruiting Judgment
Pressure test whether the person creates momentum when clarity is incomplete, or only performs well once someone else has already structured the work.
