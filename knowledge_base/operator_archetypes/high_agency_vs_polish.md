# High Agency Vs Polish

## Core Insight
Polish can create confidence in interviews while hiding dependence on structure.

## Tina Reasoning
Some candidates communicate beautifully and build trust quickly, but their operating model depends on alignment access, mature process, and clear scaffolding. The risk is not low intelligence; it is maintenance cost in chaos.

## Use When
- A candidate interviews well but feels overly managed.
- The user worries about agency, structure dependence, or executive polish.

## Recruiting Judgment
Pressure test how the person prioritizes without alignment access, creates clarity without structure, and acts when no one has handed them the lane.
