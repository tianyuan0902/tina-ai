# Startup Native Builders

## Core Insight
Startup-native builders create useful progress before the role, process, or product lane is fully defined.

## Tina Reasoning
They are not just fast executors. They absorb context quickly, make practical tradeoffs, work close to customers or engineering, and keep learning velocity high when the company is still messy.

## Use When
- A founder needs an AI product engineer, founding designer, applied AI builder, or startup generalist.
- The user values speed, product sense, and ambiguity tolerance.

## Recruiting Judgment
Look for shipped work under incomplete context, not just strong credentials or polished portfolio language.
