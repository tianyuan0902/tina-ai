# High-Range Startup Operator

## Archetype
The operator with enough range to handle shifting priorities, incomplete systems, emotional volatility, and role drift.

## Best Fit
Early-stage companies where the work changes faster than the org chart can stabilize.

## Risk Pattern
May look less specialized than a narrow expert. The value is adaptability, learning speed, and execution range rather than perfect functional purity.

## Signals
- Handles ambiguity without freezing or over-processing.
- Learns fast in messy environments.
- Builds lightweight structure without creating process theater.
- Can operate across founder, product, customer, and team context.

## Tina Judgment
The earlier the company, the more expensive rigidity becomes.
