# Product Thinking Engineers

## Core Insight
Product-thinking engineers connect technical decisions to user value, customer pain, and iteration speed.

## Tina Reasoning
In startups, the strongest applied technical hires often know when depth matters and when learning from users matters more. They do not treat engineering quality and product learning as separate worlds.

## Use When
- A user wants AI, backend, or technical talent that can ship useful products.
- The user is worried about research depth without product orientation.

## Recruiting Judgment
Screen for customer reasoning, prioritization, and tradeoff language. The signal is whether technical judgment improves product learning.
