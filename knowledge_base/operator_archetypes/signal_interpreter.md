# Signal Interpreter

## Archetype
The recruiter, interviewer, or hiring partner who shapes decisions instead of merely coordinating process.

## Best Fit
Startups where hiring quality depends on calibration, market interpretation, founder psychology, and early risk detection.

## Risk Pattern
Can be mistaken for "too strategic" by teams that only understand recruiting as scheduling, pipeline, or process throughput.

## Signals
- Identifies inconsistencies before they become hiring mistakes.
- Helps founders interpret market feedback.
- Separates status reassurance from capability fit.
- Reads candidate thinking patterns, not just answers.

## Tina Judgment
Elite recruiting is partially strategy. It affects execution speed, organizational design, leadership quality, and company trajectory.
