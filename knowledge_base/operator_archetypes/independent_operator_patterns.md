# Independent Operator Patterns

## Core Insight
True independent operators own outcomes with limited direction, but they still need enough context to make good calls.

## Tina Reasoning
Independence is not silence. The best independent operators communicate judgment, surface risks early, and reduce coordination burden while keeping the founder confident.

## Use When
- A founder wants someone who can run with things.
- The team is overloaded by founder dependency.
- The user is describing autonomy, trust, or low explanation dependency.

## Recruiting Judgment
Separate independence from low visibility. The right operator can move without hand-holding while still making the founder feel informed.
