# Product Taste Under Ambiguity

## Core Insight
Early product taste is the ability to make useful decisions before the team has complete data, clean specs, or mature process.

## Tina Reasoning
This matters for founding designers, PMs, and product-minded engineers. The person needs to choose what to clarify, what to ship, and what to ignore while staying close to customer and engineering reality.

## Use When
- A founder wants a founding designer or product-oriented builder.
- The user wants speed without superficial polish.

## Recruiting Judgment
Look for taste expressed through tradeoffs, not aesthetic preference alone.
