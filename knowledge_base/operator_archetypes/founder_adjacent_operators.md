# Founder Adjacent Operators

## Core Insight
Founder-adjacent operators sit close enough to founder context to translate ambiguity into execution.

## Tina Reasoning
They are useful when the founder has taste, urgency, and context that has not yet been codified. Their value is not only doing tasks; it is converting founder fragments into product, hiring, customer, or operating motion.

## Use When
- The role needs product taste, speed, and proximity to engineering or customers.
- A founder needs leverage but cannot fully define the lane.

## Recruiting Judgment
Look for people who can think with the founder without becoming dependent on the founder for every decision.
