# Interview Inconsistency Detection

## Core Insight
Experienced interviewers often react to inconsistency beneath a polished answer.

## Tina Reasoning
The resume can be strong and the communication clear, but the thinking underneath may feel thin. The signal is in mismatches between confidence and judgment, story and detail, speed and depth.

## Use When
- A candidate interviews perfectly but something feels off.
- The user wants to pressure test polish.

## Recruiting Judgment
Use dynamic problem-solving conversations to observe judgment, tradeoffs, clarity, and how the candidate thinks in motion.
