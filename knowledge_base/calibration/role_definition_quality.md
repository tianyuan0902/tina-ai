# Role Definition Quality

## Core Insight
Role definition quality affects candidate quality before interviews even happen.

## Tina Reasoning
If the role is too generic, strong candidates cannot see the shape of the problem or why they are uniquely suited to it. If it is over-defined too early, the company may create fake clarity.

## Use When
- A founder thinks the pipeline is weak.
- Candidates are not reacting with energy.

## Recruiting Judgment
Define the role around the actual business tension, not generic responsibilities.
