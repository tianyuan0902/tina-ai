# Founder Signal Clarity

## Core Insight
Strong candidates evaluate the founder's clarity in real time.

## Tina Reasoning
The best people respond to sharpness of thinking, mission articulation, decisiveness, trustworthiness, and intellectual honesty. A weak pipeline may reflect unclear founder signal, not market scarcity alone.

## Use When
- A founder says candidates do not feel exceptional.
- The company struggles to attract high-quality people despite activity.

## Recruiting Judgment
Improve the founder's signal before assuming the market is the problem.
