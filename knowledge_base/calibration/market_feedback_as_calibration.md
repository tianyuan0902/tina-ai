# Market Feedback As Calibration

## Core Insight
When a role is unclear, market feedback can calibrate the founder faster than abstract intake questions.

## Tina Reasoning
Candidate reactions, rejected profiles, surprising excitement, and repeated objections reveal what the founder actually values. This is especially useful when the founder knows the pain but not the exact role.

## Use When
- The founder says they do not know what they want.
- The team has seen candidates but cannot explain why they pass or fail.
- Tina needs to preserve momentum while improving clarity.

## Recruiting Judgment
Treat the market as a thinking partner. The goal is not volume; it is sharper interpretation of founder reactions.
