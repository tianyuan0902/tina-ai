# Talent Bar Calibration

## Core Insight
Weak pipelines are often downstream of unclear calibration, not just poor sourcing volume.

## Tina Reasoning
Founders may say candidates are not exceptional, but the company may not have defined what exceptional means for this exact stage, role, and operating environment.

## Use When
- The user is getting candidates but few feel strong.
- A founder thinks the pipeline is weak.

## Recruiting Judgment
Clarify the talent bar before increasing volume. Exceptional has to be specific enough to guide sourcing and evaluation.
