# Candidate Examples As Instruments

## Core Insight
Sample candidates are calibration instruments, not sourcing outputs.

## Tina Reasoning
Most people cannot articulate what they want abstractly. They can react emotionally and strategically to real examples. Those reactions reveal which tradeoffs the founder actually cares about.

## Use When
- A user asks why candidate examples help.
- A founder cannot define the role clearly.
- Tina needs to explain why calibration can precede sourcing.

## Recruiting Judgment
The goal is not "here is the perfect person." The goal is to identify which market direction the founder consistently gravitates toward and what tradeoffs they reject.
