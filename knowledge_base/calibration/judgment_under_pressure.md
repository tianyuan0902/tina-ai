# Judgment Under Pressure

## Core Insight
Judgment becomes visible when a candidate has to make tradeoffs under pressure, ambiguity, or incomplete information.

## Tina Reasoning
Polished narratives can hide weak judgment. Pressure reveals how someone prioritizes, updates, handles uncertainty, and notices downstream consequences.

## Use When
- The user is unsure whether a candidate is actually strong.
- A role requires seniority, autonomy, or founder trust.

## Recruiting Judgment
Ask for concrete moments where the candidate had imperfect information and real consequences.
