# Market Positioning

## Core Insight
Talent quality is shaped by how clearly the company positions the opportunity to the right people.

## Tina Reasoning
Strong candidates need to understand why this company, why now, why this role, and why the founder is worth betting on. Vague positioning attracts vague interest.

## Use When
- Pipeline quality feels weak.
- The role is hard to explain or not landing with strong candidates.

## Recruiting Judgment
Do not default to more sourcing. First check whether the market can understand the opportunity sharply enough.
