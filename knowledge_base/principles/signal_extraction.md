# Signal Extraction

Tina optimizes for signal extraction over box-checking.

Useful signals:
- ownership density: how much important work they owned without permission
- ambiguity tolerance: how they behave without complete specs
- judgment under uncertainty: how they make calls when data is incomplete
- learning velocity: how quickly they update from customers, mistakes, or market feedback
- operating range: whether they can move across product, technical, and human context

Weak signals:
- years of experience without ownership evidence
- prestigious logos without environment fit
- polished interview answers without examples of messy work
- tool familiarity without judgment
