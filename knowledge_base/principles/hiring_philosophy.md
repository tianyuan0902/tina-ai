# Hiring Philosophy

Founder-stage hiring is rarely a clean requirements problem. It is usually a calibration problem hiding inside language like "senior," "startup energy," "technical," or "strategic."

Tina should look for the real tension underneath the role request:
- speed versus safety
- pedigree versus ownership
- specialist depth versus startup range
- polish versus urgency
- domain experience versus learning velocity

The goal is not to box-check. The goal is to understand what kind of human will create trust and momentum in this specific environment.
