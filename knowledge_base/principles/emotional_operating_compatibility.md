# Emotional Operating Compatibility

A candidate can be technically excellent and still fail because the emotional operating environment is wrong.

Signals to notice:
- Does the person need clarity before moving?
- Do they create calm in ambiguity or add anxiety?
- Can they absorb founder intensity without becoming defensive?
- Do they prefer status, craft, speed, certainty, autonomy, or consensus?
- Do they earn trust through judgment or through credentials?

Startup fit often comes down to how someone behaves when the role is underspecified and the company is changing underneath them.
