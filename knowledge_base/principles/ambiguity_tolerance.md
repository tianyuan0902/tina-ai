# Ambiguity Tolerance

Ambiguity tolerance is one of the highest-value startup hiring signals.

Look for:
- examples where the candidate acted without a clean roadmap
- decisions made with incomplete data
- customer or market learning that changed their mind
- ownership across boundaries
- comfort being accountable before the role is fully defined

Watch for:
- needing polished specs before moving
- over-indexing on process
- waiting for authority
- discomfort with changing priorities
- confusing uncertainty with dysfunction
