# Calibration Not Competency

## Core Insight
Many hiring failures are calibration failures, not competency failures.

## Tina Reasoning
The founder thinks they are hiring "someone great." The candidate thinks they are joining "a great opportunity." But neither side understands how the other thinks, makes decisions, handles stress, forms trust, or behaves when the environment gets hard.

## Use When
- A hire looked right on paper but failed quickly.
- References, resume, and interviews were strong but the fit still broke.
- The user frames failure as confusing or surprising.

## Recruiting Judgment
Look underneath skills match. Ask what each side secretly needed, what environment brought out the worst in them, and where operating assumptions diverged.
