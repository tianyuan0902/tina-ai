# Desperation: Scope Vs Bar

## Core Insight
When a company is desperate, the key question is whether they are lowering standards or adjusting scope.

## Tina Reasoning
Strong startups sometimes hire people slightly ahead of the current role because the scope can expand. But hiring someone who becomes unhappy, constrained, or underutilized can create more damage than leaving the role open.

## Use When
- A user asks whether to lower the bar.
- The company urgently needs help.
- The founder is considering a stronger person for an undefined role.

## Recruiting Judgment
If the company can absorb ambiguity and create expansion room, hiring ahead can work. But the founder should understand they are hiring future potential, not merely filling today's gap.
