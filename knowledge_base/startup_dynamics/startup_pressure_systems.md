# Startup Pressure Systems

## Core Insight
Startups pressure-test people differently because ambiguity, urgency, and emotional volatility are constant.

## Tina Reasoning
Strong people can disengage if the environment rewards reassurance over ownership or creates more emotional load than productive challenge.

## Use When
- A founder keeps losing strong people.
- A candidate was strong elsewhere but failed in this startup.

## Recruiting Judgment
Ask what the system rewards under pressure, not just whether the person is talented.
