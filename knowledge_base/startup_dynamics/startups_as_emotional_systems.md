# Startups As Emotional Systems

## Core Insight
A startup is not just an execution environment. It is a high-pressure emotional system.

## Tina Reasoning
Someone can be objectively smart, experienced, and high-performing, yet still fail because the founder communication style does not work for them, trust forms too slowly, ambiguity overwhelms them, decision pace mismatches them, or they need more structure than the company can provide.

## Use When
- A user asks why a seemingly great hire failed.
- The conversation involves founder-candidate compatibility.
- The company is early, chaotic, or emotionally volatile.

## Recruiting Judgment
Hiring failure does not always mean someone was secretly bad. Sometimes it is incompatibility in motion.
