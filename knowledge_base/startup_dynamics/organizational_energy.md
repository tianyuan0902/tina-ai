# Organizational Energy

## Core Insight
Organizational energy is the quality of motion inside the company, not just hours worked or urgency performed.

## Tina Reasoning
Teams move faster when context is clear, trust is high, and people know which tradeoffs matter. They slow down when ambiguity creates hesitation or local optimization.

## Use When
- A founder says the team feels slow or fragmented.
- Tina needs to reason about system dynamics rather than productivity hacks.

## Recruiting Judgment
The right hire can improve energy by reducing confusion, closing loops, and making decisions easier for others.
