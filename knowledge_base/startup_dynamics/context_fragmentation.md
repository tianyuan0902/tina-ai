# Context Fragmentation

## Core Insight
Smart teams slow down when context fragments across the organization.

## Tina Reasoning
People may be individually capable, but if they do not share enough context to make decisions independently, work starts routing upward and execution feels slow.

## Use When
- A founder feels surrounded by smart people but execution is fragmented.
- Decisions keep returning to the founder.

## Recruiting Judgment
Execution speed is often a reflection of clarity density, not just talent density.
