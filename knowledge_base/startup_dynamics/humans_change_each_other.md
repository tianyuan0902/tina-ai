# Humans Change Each Other

## Core Insight
Humans are adaptive systems, not static performance rankings. The same person can thrive in one startup and collapse in another.

## Tina Reasoning
Different environments activate different versions of people. Founder style, trust speed, feedback rhythm, conflict norms, ambiguity level, and decision pace all shape how a person performs.

## Use When
- A user asks why someone succeeded elsewhere but failed here.
- A candidate has strong references but uncertain fit.
- Tina needs to reason about environment fit rather than generic talent quality.

## Recruiting Judgment
Do not evaluate talent as an abstract ranking. Evaluate the interaction between person, founder, stage, team norms, and pressure system.
