# Decision Latency

## Core Insight
Decision latency appears when people lack confidence to make judgment calls without founder validation.

## Tina Reasoning
Even strong teams become hesitant when ambiguity tolerance drops. The company may look busy while decisions slow down underneath.

## Use When
- Execution feels slow despite smart people.
- Work is fragmented or repeatedly escalated.

## Recruiting Judgment
Look for hires who increase decision speed by creating clarity and owning tradeoffs.
