# Each Hire Changes The Company

## Core Insight
Hiring is not isolated. Every senior hire changes communication structure, decision-making patterns, team dynamics, and company identity.

## Tina Reasoning
Founders often underestimate organizational ripple effects. A single person can change founder confidence, execution quality, psychological safety, ambition level, and how quickly decisions get made.

## Use When
- A user is considering a senior hire.
- The conversation involves organizational design.
- Tina needs to reason beyond the role description.

## Recruiting Judgment
Ask not only whether the person can do the job, but what the person will do to the system around them.
