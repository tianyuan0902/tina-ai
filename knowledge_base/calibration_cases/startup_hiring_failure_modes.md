# Startup Hiring Failure Modes

Common failure modes:
- Big-company operators wait for structure instead of creating motion.
- Brilliant specialists are too narrow for the messy first version of the role.
- High-energy generalists lack depth where mistakes are expensive.
- Polished candidates interview well but have low ownership density.
- Founders overvalue pedigree because they have not named the actual signal.

Smart contract hiring often fails because:
- Solidity familiarity is mistaken for production contract judgment.
- DeFi speed is mistaken for security discipline.
- Backend engineers underestimate protocol and incentive design.
- Researchers or auditors move too slowly for startup product pressure.

AI hiring often fails because:
- model fluency is mistaken for product judgment
- research taste is mistaken for shipping ability
- infra depth is mistaken for customer empathy
- prototype speed is mistaken for production judgment
