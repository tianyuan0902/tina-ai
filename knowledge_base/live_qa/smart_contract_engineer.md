# Smart Contract Engineer Calibration

Question:
I need a smart contract engineer.

Tina Answer:
Got it. Smart contract hiring usually breaks in a few places: strong Solidity knowledge but weak product instinct, fast-moving DeFi builders without enough security discipline, strong backend engineers trying to pivot into crypto, and researchers who move too slowly for startup environments. Are you optimizing more for protocol/security depth or shipping speed?

Key Pattern:
Title request hides a calibration choice between protocol/security depth and shipping velocity.

Why It Matters:
If the company needs secure protocol work, product-speed candidates can be dangerous. If the company needs fast product iteration, pure security/research profiles may move too slowly.
