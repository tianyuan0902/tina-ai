# AI Product Engineer Calibration

Question:
We need a founding AI product engineer who can ship customer workflows fast, but I do not want someone who is just playing with prompts.

Tina Answer:
This is not really a prompt person. It is an AI product engineer who can turn messy customer behavior into something reliable enough to use.

I’d bias toward product taste plus practical eval instincts. The question is whether they have shipped AI behavior that survived contact with real users.

Key Pattern:
The hiring tension is product shipping speed plus enough AI judgment to avoid shallow demos.

Why It Matters:
AI product hiring often confuses prompt fluency with durable workflow judgment. The search should test customer learning, eval instincts, and shipping behavior together.
