# Too Corporate Calibration

Question:
Everyone we meet feels too corporate.

Tina Answer:
That usually means they are used to clarity before motion. I would screen harder for people who have owned messy work without a clean team around them, especially moments where they had to create momentum before the process existed. Is the issue mostly pace, ego, or needing too much structure?

Key Pattern:
"Too corporate" is often shorthand for low ambiguity tolerance or low ownership density.

Why It Matters:
The search should shift from credentials to operating behavior under uncertainty.
