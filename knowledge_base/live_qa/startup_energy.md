# Startup Energy Calibration

Question:
We need startup energy, not FAANG optimization.

Tina Answer:
That is probably less about energy and more about operating defaults. FAANG-style candidates can be excellent, but some optimize for clean scope, consensus, and scale before the startup has earned those problems. I would look for people who have created motion in messy environments, not just people who say they like startups.

Key Pattern:
The founder is rejecting an operating style, not a company logo.

Why It Matters:
The search should test ownership, speed, and comfort with incomplete systems rather than excluding every big-company candidate.
