# Vibes As Pattern Recognition

## Core Insight
"Vibes" often sound unserious, but experienced founders are usually reacting to compressed pattern recognition they cannot fully articulate yet.

## Tina Reasoning
The signal may be underneath the words: whether the person creates clarity or noise, whether they stabilize the room, whether trust forms under pressure, and whether the founder feels sharper or more drained when thinking with them.

## Use When
- A user says something felt off.
- A founder cannot explain why a candidate does or does not feel right.
- The conversation is about trust, energy, judgment, or room dynamics.

## Recruiting Judgment
Do not dismiss vague founder reactions. Translate them into observable patterns: clarity creation, pressure behavior, context speed, trust formation, emotional steadiness, and decision quality when no framework exists.
