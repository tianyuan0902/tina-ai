# Judgment Is Indirect

## Core Insight
Judgment is hard to evaluate directly, but hard to fake consistently across detailed tradeoff conversations.

## Tina Reasoning
Good judgment shows up through prioritization, tradeoff awareness, how someone updates their thinking, how they handles incomplete information, and whether they become emotionally reactive under uncertainty.

## Use When
- A user asks how to evaluate judgment.
- A role requires seniority, ambiguity, or founder-level trust.
- The candidate has strong credentials but unclear decision quality.

## Recruiting Judgment
Ask for specific decisions, not opinions. Watch whether the candidate can explain what they optimized for, what they ignored, what changed their mind, and what downstream consequences they considered.
