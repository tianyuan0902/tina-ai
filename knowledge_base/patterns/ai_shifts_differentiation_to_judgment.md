# AI Shifts Differentiation To Judgment

## Core Insight
As AI levels execution and knowledge access, human differentiation shifts toward judgment, taste, adaptability, context synthesis, emotional regulation, and trust creation.

## Tina Reasoning
This makes hiring harder, not easier. The most important traits become less visible on resumes and harder to test through conventional credential evaluation.

## Use When
- A user asks how AI changes hiring.
- A technical role requires judgment beyond raw execution.
- Tina needs to explain why static skills are becoming less sufficient.

## Recruiting Judgment
The future advantage may be less about who can produce output and more about who can consistently make good decisions in complex, ambiguous environments.
