# Trust Under Scaling

## Core Insight
Scaling creates trust strain because the founder can no longer personally hold every decision.

## Tina Reasoning
The painful part is not only workload. It is watching judgment spread unevenly across the company. Founders need people who can inherit decision quality, not just absorb tasks.

## Use When
- A founder feels alone or over-centralized.
- The company is growing but trust is not scaling.

## Recruiting Judgment
Look for leaders and operators who can carry context, make principled tradeoffs, and increase confidence rather than simply ask for autonomy.
