# Executive Calibration

## Core Insight
Executive calibration is about shared judgment, not just seniority.

## Tina Reasoning
Founders often need leaders who can see the business with enough depth to make decisions that feel coherent with company reality. This takes context, trust, and repeated reasoning quality.

## Use When
- A founder feels nobody sees the business deeply enough.
- The user is considering senior leadership or founder-adjacent operators.

## Recruiting Judgment
Screen for how the person absorbs context, updates their model, and reasons through second-order consequences.
