# Founder Mirror Hiring

## Core Insight
When founders say they want someone who thinks like them, they often want speed of context absorption more than true psychological sameness.

## Tina Reasoning
Hiring a mirror can feel efficient, but it may duplicate the founder's strengths while leaving operational blind spots untouched. The better target is shared judgment, low explanation dependency, and complementary ownership.

## Use When
- A founder says they need a clone.
- The user wants someone who can run without constant explanation.

## Recruiting Judgment
Reframe from "thinks exactly like me" to "can infer intent, prioritize well, and challenge gaps without creating drag."
