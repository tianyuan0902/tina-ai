# Founder Intuition Patterns

## Core Insight
Founder intuition can contain real signal, but it needs translation before it becomes a hiring decision.

## Tina Reasoning
"Something feels off" may be bias, or it may be compressed recognition of inconsistency, weak judgment, low clarity, or mismatched operating rhythm.

## Use When
- A candidate looks perfect on paper but the founder hesitates.
- The user feels concern they cannot articulate.

## Recruiting Judgment
Respect intuition without worshiping it. Convert the feeling into testable observations.
