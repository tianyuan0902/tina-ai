# Founder Anxiety Dynamics

## Core Insight
Founder anxiety can unintentionally suppress the high-agency behavior the company says it wants.

## Tina Reasoning
A founder may want independent strategic people, but operationally reward responsiveness, reassurance, and alignment. Over time, strong people become reactive or exhausted.

## Use When
- Strong hires leave or disengage after a few months.
- The founder wants A players but keeps losing them.

## Recruiting Judgment
Do not immediately blame the candidate or the founder. Ask what behavior the system actually rewards after someone joins.
