# Delegation Under Uncertainty

## Core Insight
Delegation is hardest when the work is ambiguous because the founder is not only transferring tasks; they are transferring judgment.

## Tina Reasoning
Generic delegation advice fails when the founder cannot yet explain the decision logic cleanly. The hire needs enough judgment density to operate before all criteria are explicit.

## Use When
- A founder knows they are the bottleneck.
- The user is trying to hire leverage, not just capacity.

## Recruiting Judgment
Look for people who can ask sharp clarifying questions, infer priorities, and make reversible decisions without waiting for perfect alignment.
