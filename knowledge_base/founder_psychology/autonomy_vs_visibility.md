# Autonomy Vs Visibility

## Core Insight
Autonomy and visibility are different needs that founders often collapse into one request.

## Tina Reasoning
Some founders say they want autonomous people but feel unsafe when they cannot see enough motion. Strong hires can become frustrated if visibility needs turn into hidden management overhead.

## Use When
- A founder keeps losing independent people.
- A user describes tension around responsiveness, reassurance, or visibility.

## Recruiting Judgment
Clarify the operating contract: how much visibility creates trust without destroying autonomy?
