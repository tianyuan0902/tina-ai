# Context Density

## Core Insight
Founders often operate with much higher context density than the rest of the team.

## Tina Reasoning
Context density includes customer nuance, investor pressure, product history, team capability, cash constraints, competitive risk, and founder intuition. Teams slow down when that context is not transferable.

## Use When
- A founder feels nobody thinks deeply enough.
- The company is slowing down because decisions route through the founder.

## Recruiting Judgment
The hiring need may be someone who can absorb context quickly and convert it into independent judgment.
