# Founder Dependency Loops

## Core Insight
Companies slow down when every decision routes through the founder because only the founder has enough context and trust.

## Tina Reasoning
This is usually less about delegation tactics and more about a founder-dependency problem. The founder becomes the fastest judgment loop, so the system keeps returning to them. A useful hire can take messy context, make a call, and keep the team moving without pulling the founder into every decision.

## Use When
- The user says everything routes through them.
- The company is slowing down because decisions are centralized.
- The user says they need a PM, operator, or lead who can make the team less dependent on them.

## Recruiting Judgment
Identify where trust breaks: priorities, customer signal, product decisions, team alignment, or execution details. Screen for independent judgment and clarity creation, not just roadmap ownership or coordination polish.
