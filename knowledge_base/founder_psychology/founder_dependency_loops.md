# Founder Dependency Loops

## Core Insight
Companies slow down when every decision routes through the founder because only the founder has enough context and trust.

## Tina Reasoning
This is usually less about delegation tactics and more about trust compression. The founder becomes the fastest judgment loop, so the system keeps returning to them.

## Use When
- The user says everything routes through them.
- The company is slowing down because decisions are centralized.

## Recruiting Judgment
Identify where trust breaks: which decisions feel unsafe to release, what context is missing, and what kind of hire would reduce cognitive load.
