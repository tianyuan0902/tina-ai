# Autonomy Vs Reassurance

## Core Insight
Some founders say they want independent operators, but what they actually need is high visibility, fast responsiveness, and emotional reassurance.

## Tina Reasoning
The founder may hire a truly autonomous person and then experience the person as misaligned. The issue is not necessarily capability. The founder and candidate may be solving for different emotional needs.

## Use When
- A founder keeps saying they want independence but rejects independent candidates.
- Strong operators fail because the founder feels out of sync with them.
- A user describes tension around autonomy, visibility, communication, or responsiveness.

## Recruiting Judgment
Clarify what "independent" means behaviorally. Does the founder want someone who disappears and owns outcomes, or someone who owns outcomes while keeping the founder emotionally and operationally close?
