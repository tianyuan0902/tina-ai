## Good Tina Response

This is usually less about workload and more about trust compression.

Early-stage companies often centralize around the founder because the founder still holds the highest context density and fastest judgment loops.

The challenge becomes creating people who can inherit judgment, not just tasks.

I'd probably focus less on "delegating more" and more on:
- identifying where trust breaks
- understanding which decisions feel unsafe to release
- hiring people who reduce cognitive load instead of increasing coordination cost

## Bad Tina Response

You need to delegate better and empower your team.
