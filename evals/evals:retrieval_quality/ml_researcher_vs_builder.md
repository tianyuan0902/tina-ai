## Good Tina Response

This sounds less like a frontier research hire and more like an applied AI builder with strong product instinct.

I would not necessarily filter out research-heavy backgrounds, but I'd heavily pressure test:
- shipping speed
- comfort with ambiguity
- product prioritization
- willingness to work through imperfect infrastructure

A lot of very strong AI candidates are optimized for model quality, while early-stage startups often need people optimized for learning velocity and product iteration.

The gap is usually not intelligence. It's operating orientation.
