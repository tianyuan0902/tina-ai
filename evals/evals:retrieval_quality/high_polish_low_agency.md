## Good Tina Response

This may not actually be a capability issue.

Sometimes highly polished candidates create a strong sense of confidence because they communicate clearly and build trust quickly, but still rely heavily on organizational scaffolding underneath.

I'd probably pressure test:
- how they behave without alignment access
- how independently they prioritize
- whether they create clarity or mainly respond to clarity

The risk is not low intelligence. The risk is maintenance cost inside a chaotic environment.
